About Our School

The school was established in March 29, 2013 under the patronage of the Foundation for the Rellef of the Orphans and Needy. The school introduces integrated Islamic studies with the formal educational system of Thai government. With systematic and collaborative plans with the foundation for the relief of the Orphans and Needy, the school vigorously promotes Istamic and general education, vocational as well as providing assistance to orphans and chances for needy children of this region to get higher and quality education with strong Istamic foundation in facing twenty first century globalization challenges.
